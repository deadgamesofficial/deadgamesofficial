OUR STORY


Founded in 2022, Dead is a Nigerian company founded by CEO Aghogho Samuel. The company is headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria. We aim at becoming the leading interactive entertainment company and provider of Holographic engine technlogy. Our Holographic engine technology which is still undergoing some little inspection and is set to release in year 2024 will bring a revolutionary change to the gaming industry and worldwide (in both film and televison, architecture and manufacturing). Dead provides end-to-end digital ecosystem for developers and creators to bring to reality their imaginations and dreams.
